Upload a Match schedule

Instructions:

Step 1: Go to the Blue Alliance website and select the event.

Step 2. Right-click on the match schedule webpage and select 'View Page Source'.

Step 3. On the 'page source' view, select all of the content (Ctrl-a) and copy it (Ctrl-c)

Step 4. Connect to the scouting app and come to this page.

Step 5. Paste the content (Ctrl-v) in the text area below and click on 'Upload Event'.
